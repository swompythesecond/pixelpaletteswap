What Is PixelPaletteSwap?

PixelPaletteSwap is a free, browser-based tool that lets you instantly swap colors in pixel art images and GIF animations. Whether you're creating character variants, experimenting with different color schemes, or batch-processing game assets, our tool makes palette manipulation quick and intuitive.

Unlike traditional image editors that require complex workflows to change colors, PixelPaletteSwap is purpose-built for pixel art. Simply upload your image, click on a color, choose a replacement, and watch every pixel of that color change across all frames instantly.

Best of all, everything happens right in your browser. Your images never leave your computer‚Äîall processing is done locally using JavaScript, ensuring complete privacy and zero upload wait times.

The Problem We Solve

Creating color variants of pixel art has traditionally been tedious. In most image editors, you need to manually select colors using magic wand tools, deal with anti-aliasing edge cases, and repeat the process for every single frame of an animation. For a 60-frame walk cycle with 4 color variants, that's potentially hundreds of manual operations.

PixelPaletteSwap solves this by treating your pixel art as data. We extract the color palette, let you remap colors, and apply changes to all frames simultaneously. What used to take hours now takes seconds.

Need to create 5 different team colors for your game's characters? Upload once, swap colors 5 times, export 5 GIFs. Done.
