Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how PixelPaletteSwap.com ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website and pixel art palette swap tool.

Information We Collect

PixelPaletteSwap is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Image Data Collection: All image processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your images are never uploaded to our servers. We cannot see, store, or access any images you edit using our tool.
  • No Account Required: We don't require user accounts, so we don't collect personal information like names, email addresses, or passwords.
  • Automatically Collected Information: Like most websites, our web servers may collect standard log information such as IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps for security and analytics purposes.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use analytics services to understand how visitors use our website, which helps us improve the user experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: We use third-party advertising services, including Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our website and tool
  • Improve and optimize the user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Protect against fraud and abuse

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except:

  • To trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website (such as hosting providers)
  • To advertising partners as described above
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have about you.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising using the links provided above.
  • Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For California residents, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
